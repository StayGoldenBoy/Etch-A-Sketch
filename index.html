Welcome to Etch-a-Sketch

Press "Start" to begin. You will be prompted to choose a grid size first. Once the size is selected you can begin to drag your cursor across the squares to change the color. Just like a real Etch-a-Sketch you cant stop in the middle and move to the outside, It's continuous! Press "New Grid" if you want a different amount of squares in the box. (Max 100 x 100).
